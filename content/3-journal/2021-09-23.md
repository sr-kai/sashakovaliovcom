Body seems to be tired and that has an effect on the quality of practice.

But this is inevitable – body is part of the physical world that is constantly changing. We can't expect it remain in the same condition day after day. Is there attachment to feeling well? To things being easier?

I'm offering myself kindness and compassion – dukkha is real. Can I see past it?
